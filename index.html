About Us

Sarah Glass Ceramics is a women-led ceramics studio in Fort Lauderdale. Our mission is to provide a welcoming and supportive environment for people of all skill levels to explore the art of ceramics. We are happy to offer space and equipment to help you unleash your inner artist.
